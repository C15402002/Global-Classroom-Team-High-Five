General Information

With more than 550 locations across six continents, Hilton Hotels & Resorts provide an authentic and contemporary experience for guests worldwide. In the vibrant city of Belfast you can visit a pub along the Golden Mile or enjoy shopping in Donegall Place. Stroll through museums in University Quarter or enjoy the gothic beauty of cathedrals. In friendly Dublin, tour the Guinness Storehouse or take a walk on St Stephen's Green. Looking for serenity? Book the Hilton hotel in Templepatrick on the private grounds of the Castle Upton Estate. No matter what appeals on the Emerald Isle, Hilton hotels in Ireland are ready to welcome you in with legendary hospitality. We are Hilton team members who love to travel the world and show off the hidden gems in our hometowns.
